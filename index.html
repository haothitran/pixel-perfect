What are these numbers?

These two numbers represent your current browser size dimensions (width x height) in pixels. Knowing your brower size will help you to design websites for different screens. Especially if it is imporant to have certain elements of your website displayed above the fold to immediately catch your audience's eyes. Values will live update when you resize your browser window.
